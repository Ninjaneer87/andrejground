About me

As a pioneer in this area, my value is not yet in my skills, but rather in the potential, curiosity, and a strong urge to learn new things daily.
Creating something from nothing, solving all kind of problems from macro to micro, makes me feel fulfilled and productive.
UI and UX are one of the most important aspects of modern programming, in my opinion, so I tend to make my projects as user friendly as I am capable of.
Beside that, I am equally interested in learning how back-end works, and it is definitely on my roadmap.
My final goal, as programming is concerned, is to become a full-stack developer.
